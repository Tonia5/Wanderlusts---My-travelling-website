LET'S EXPLORE THE WORLD
Ready for new adventures?

Cappadocia

A very special and beloved trip that combines in a unique way the breathtaking rocky landscape, the various activities and the special flavors of the traditional dishes. A trip that definitely deserves to be on the list of places you must visit!

Zanzibar

A destination that will never fade from our memory. Exotic sandy beaches with a colorful seabeds, happy friendly local people and delicious refreshing fruit-smoothies are just a few out of the many things we will always recall...

Mauritius

Every time we hear the word "Mauritius" amazing beaches, swimming with sea creatures, hiking at high altitudes with dazzling views, beautiful temples and delicious food come to our mind! What else do you need to be convinced?

Tenerife

A surprisingly wonderful trip where in just a few days you can see so many different landscapes. Black beaches, volcanos and deserts that look like the Wild West, mountains for climbing and delicious Spanish food are few from many things you can meet there!

Dubrovnik

If you want to travel back in time and live for a while inside a castle with so many beautiful cobblestones, admiring the historical landscape around you, feeling like you're living in another era and of course if you are a fan of "Game of Thrones" you should not miss this trip!

Japan

Definitely a dream trip. Unique landscapes that combine evolution with tradition. A whole different world, that surprised us every moment with its amazing details. Japanese temples, shrines, wonderful nature, huge dazzling buildings; Images that will stay forever in your mind!
